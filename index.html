01 — Ambassador Program

Building a $407M Growth Engine from Zero

Inherited a blank canvas at MPOWER Financing. No playbook, no team, no existing ambassador base. Designed the entire program architecture — recruitment funnels, onboarding flows, incentive structures, gamification mechanics, and performance tracking — from first principles. Scaled to 400+ active ambassadors spanning Africa, Southeast Asia, and South Asia within 2 years.

$407M in loan application volume generated
400+ ambassadors across 20 countries

02 — Revenue Growth

$5M to $40M in 24 Months

Through compounding improvements across ambassador recruitment, campaign optimisation, lifecycle marketing, and cross-functional funnel execution, grew MPOWER's disbursed loan revenue 8x in two years. Each year built on the structural gains of the previous one — not a spike, a system.

$5M → $30M in Year 1
$30M → $40M in Year 2
8x total growth in 24 months
03 — International Expansion

20 Countries. Zero Blueprint.

Designed and executed market-entry strategies for 20 international student markets with no precedent to follow. Each market required a distinct approach to ambassador recruitment, cultural incentive design, and trust-building. Africa and Southeast Asia became the highest-growth corridors, contributing disproportionately to loan volume growth.

20 countries activated across 4 continents
Africa + SEA highest-growth loan corridors
0 → $407M pipeline from international markets
04 — Data & Dashboards

Turning Data into Decisions

Built 10 end-to-end Tableau and Google Analytics dashboards and 100+ Salesforce reports giving affiliate, engagement, and leadership teams a single real-time source of truth. Eliminated reporting lag from days to minutes. Every strategic decision in the ambassador program was backed by data built in-house.

10 Tableau + Google Analytics dashboards
100+ Salesforce reports owned

05 — Affiliate Program Management

From 40% to 56% Approval Rate

Designed and launched a priority service queue for affiliates that re-engineered the loan approval workflow end-to-end. Paired with monthly gamification campaigns and tiered incentive systems. At Phablecare, managed 14+ affiliate accounts and prevented Rs.4Cr in losses through proactive anomaly detection.

40% → 56% loan approval rate
Rs.4Cr in losses prevented
